All the profiles can be modified by selecting attributes in the drop-down lists and by
checking or not check boxes, and subsequenlty will tune the sound attributes of the box.

. The dynamic profile is visually asociated with the global shape of the box. Auditively, it
describes to the volume variation of a sound.

. The rythm profile is visually associated with the up (dashes) and bottom lines of the box
(dashes and vertical line). Auditively, it describes both global and internal rythms of the sound.

. The melodic profile is visually associated to the left part of the box (horizontal line/curve)
Auditively, it describes the evolution and consistance of sound's pitch.

. The harmonic profile is visually associated to the scheme in the center of the box.
Autitively, it describes the consistance and evolution of the harmony.
