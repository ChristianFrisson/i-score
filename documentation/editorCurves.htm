. Each tab is related to a curve describing values progression
for a specific message.

. You can switch between curves by clicking on tabs.

. To add a point, click anywhere on the draw.

. To move vertically and horizontally a point, drag and drop it.

. To move vertically a point, press Control Key and slide
vertically the point.

. To bend a curve's sub-part, press Maj Key, click on the
last point of the subcurve and drag up or down.

. To delete a point, press Alt key and click on the point.
